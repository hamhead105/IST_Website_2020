Level 3, Problem 2:

Blood type is a classification of blood based on the presence or absence of A and/or B antigens on the surface of red blood cells. You may have heard of A, B, AB, and O types.
The classification also considers the Rh factor which could be positive or negative. For this question, every blood type is either Rh positive or negative. That is, blood type could be A+ or O-, etc.
Blood type compatibility is important for blood donations as incompatible types will trigger an immune response.

  • Each blood group can donate to someone of the same group
  • Blood type O can donate to all other groups
  • Both blood types A and B can donate to blood type AB
  • Rh negative can donation to either Rh negative or positive
  • Rh positive can only donate to Rh positive
